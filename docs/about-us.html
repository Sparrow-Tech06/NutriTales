About NutriTales

last Updated: June 27, 2025

NutriTales is an educational app designed to teach people about foods — especially fruits, vegetables, dry-fruits, and herbs — in a fun, friendly and easy-to-understand way. We combine bite-sized facts, simple stories, quizzes and badges to make learning about healthy food enjoyable for users of all ages.

Our Vision

We believe learning about nutrition should be playful and accessible. Our vision is to spread health awareness and encourage healthy habits by turning facts into stories and lessons into little adventures.

Why we built NutriTales

I developed NutriTales as a one-person project to create an approachable resource for families, kids, teachers and anyone curious about the foods they eat. The app mixes research-based information with friendly storytelling so users — young and old — can learn and remember better.

Who is NutriTales for?

NutriTales is free and designed for everyone: children, parents, students and health-conscious users. Content is presented simply so it is suitable for a wide audience.

Features you’ll find:
  • Category pages for Fruits, Vegetables, Dry-fruits and Herbs
  • Short stories and simple facts for each item
  • Quizzes and a badge system for gamified learning
  • Three language options: English, বাংলা (Bangla) and हिन्दी (Hindi)
