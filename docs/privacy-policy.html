NutriTales

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 01 October 2025

1. Introduction

NutriTales respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your choices regarding that data.

2. Data we collect

At present, NutriTales does not require user accounts and does not collect personal information such as name or email for normal usage. The app may store non-personal data locally on the device (local storage) such as:

  • Quiz progress and scores (local storage)
  • Badge progress and unlocked items (local storage)
  • Language preference and app settings (local storage)

3. Analytics & Ads

Currently, NutriTales does not include analytics or advertisements. In the future we may add analytics tools (for app improvement) or ads to support development. If we add analytics or ads, we will update this policy and notify users within the app.

4. Third-party content

We use icons and images from third-party providers (for example Bootstrap Icons and Icons8). These assets are used according to their respective licenses. Third-party providers may have their own privacy practices.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

The app uses local storage on the user's device to save preferences, quiz state and similar data. We do not use web cookies beyond standard browser behavior for the web version.

6. Children

NutriTales targets users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have unintentionally collected personal data about a child, contact us and we will promptly address the request.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be announced within the app and this page will show the latest effective date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact: support@nutritales.app.

Note: This privacy summary is based on the current functionality (no authentication, local storage only). If you later add server-based accounts, analytics or advertising, this policy must be revised accordingly.
